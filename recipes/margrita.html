Meadarita

A sweet take on the traditional Margarita.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2oz tequila
  • 1 1/2oz freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2oz of Orange Blossom Mead
  • Ice
  • Lime wedge for garnish
  • Salt for rim

Steps

  1. In cocktail shaker, add tequila, lime juice and mead. Fill shaker with ice. Shake with feeling for 20 secs.
  2. Moisten rim with lime, then press in plate of salt.
  3. Fill glass with ice then strain contents of shaker into glass and garnish with lime.
