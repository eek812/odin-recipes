Salty Dog

A tart, refreshing citrus cocktail made with gin or vodak and grapefruit juice.

Ingredients

  • 6oz fresh grapefruit juice
  • 6oz grapefruit la croix
  • 3oz vodka or gin
  • 2/3oz lime juice
  • salt for rim

Steps

  1. Salt the rim of 2 glasses.
  2. Add the fresh grapefruit juice, vodka, and lime juice to a cocktail shaker and Shake!!
  3. Fill cups with ice and evenly pour cocktail over ice. Top with grapefruit la croix.
